Case Study

Hotel Booking Analytics & Cancellation Prediction

An end-to-end analytics project using SQL, Python, Power BI, and machine learning to understand hotel operations and predict booking cancellations.

119,390Raw records
86,639Clean records
27.7%Cancellation rate
81.8%XGBoost accuracy

Problem

Hotels lose revenue and operational efficiency when cancellations are not understood. Management needs to know which customers are likely to cancel, what months are risky, and what actions can reduce cancellation risk.

My Approach

  • Built SQL reporting views for business questions.
  • Cleaned and prepared hotel booking data using Python.
  • Performed EDA for seasonality and cancellation behavior.
  • Created predictive models using Logistic Regression and XGBoost.
  • Built Power BI dashboard for cancellation insights.
Insights

Key findings

August Peak

August had the highest booking volume and highest average daily rate.

Room Upgrades

Room changes/upgrades strongly reduced cancellation risk.

Special Requests

Guests with more special requests were more committed and less likely to cancel.

Model Performance

XGBoost outperformed Logistic Regression with 81.8% accuracy.
